HOW TO PLAY

Guess the Segmentle in four tries.

Each guess must be a valid 30-segment letter, number, or symbol (A-Z, a-z, 0-9, !, @, $, %, ^, &, *, (, ), [, ], {, }, /, \, ?).

You can either input the guess by clicking the individual segments or by entering a character in the text box and pressing enter.

Click Guess to submit your guess.

After each guess, the color of the segments on the result will change to show how close your guess was to the character.

If a segment is green, it is in the character.

If a segment is dark grey, it is not in the character.
